## D-Day Countdown

A prominent countdown to your **most important exam**. It displays days, hours, minutes, and seconds with color-coded urgency (Red/Amber/Green).

### Setting Your D-Day

<Steps>
  <Step icon="person-walking" title="Go to Exams">
    Navigate to the [Exams page](/users/pages/exams).
  </Step>
  <Step icon="pointer" title="Select Exam">
    Create a new exam or click on an existing one.
  </Step>
  <Step icon="flag-checkered" title="Mark as D-Day">
    Toggle the option to mark it as "D-Day" (primary exam).
  </Step>
  <Step icon="check" title="You're Set!">
    It automatically appears on your dashboard with a large countdown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips & Tricks

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="wand-magic-sparkles" title="Maximize Your Dashboard">
    1. **Check Daily** - Make checking the dashboard part of your morning routine.
    2. **Act on AI Insights** - The suggestions are personalized just for you.
    3. **Use the Cards** - Each card links to detailed pages.
    4. **Customize Layout** - Adjust dashboard density in settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="palette" title="Layout Options">
    Navigate to **Settings \> Personalization** to change:

    - **Compact:** More cards visible, less detail.
    - **Comfortable:** Balanced (recommended).
    - **Spacious:** Fewer cards, more breathing room.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="keyboard" title="Keyboard Navigation">
    - Use `Arrow Keys` to navigate between cards.
    - Press `Enter` to open selected card.
    - Press `Escape` to go back.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
