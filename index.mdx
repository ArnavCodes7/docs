## Creating Your Profile

The first step is creating your personal profile. This helps Isotope AI personalize your experience.

**What You'll Need:**
1. **Display Name** - How you want to be known in the community.
2. **Avatar** - Choose from preset avatars or customize your color.

<Tip>
  **Pro Tip:** Your display name will be visible if you join study groups and participate in community challenges. Choose something you're comfortable sharing!
</Tip>

---

## Setting Up Academic Information

This section helps Isotope AI understand your academic context:

### Grade/Class
Select your current academic level (e.g., Class 9-12, College, etc.).

### Target Exams
Choose the exams you're preparing for:
- **JEE Main & Advanced** - India's premier engineering entrance
- **NEET** - Medical entrance exams
- **CBSE Board Exams**
- **State Board Exams**
- **Custom** - Add your own exams

### Stream/Subject Combination
Select your stream:
- **Science (PCM)** - Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
- **Science (PCB)** - Physics, Chemistry, Biology
- **Commerce**
- **Arts/Humanities**

<Note>
  **Why this matters:** Based on your target exams, Isotope AI can automatically load relevant syllabi and suggest study plans tailored to your goals.
</Note>

## Defining Study Goals

Set your daily study targets to help Isotope AI track your progress:

### Daily Study Hours
Choose how many hours you plan to study each day:
- **Casual (2-3 hours)** - Light study routine
- **Moderate (4-5 hours)** - Standard preparation
- **Intense (6-8 hours)** - Serious exam preparation
- **Extreme (8+ hours)** - Full-time study mode

### Subject Priorities
Rank your subjects by importance:
- Primary focus subjects (highest priority)
- Secondary subjects
- Subjects needing more attention

<Tip>
  You can adjust these goals anytime from Settings. Isotope AI uses these goals to calculate your daily progress and suggest optimizations.
</Tip>

## Loading Exam Syllabi

One of Isotope AI's most powerful features is the ability to load professional exam syllabi directly into your Study plan.

### Available Templates
- **JEE (Joint Entrance Examination):** Main & Advanced with complete syllabus for all chapters and topics.
- **NEET:** Full PCB syllabus for medical college admissions.
- **CBSE:** Class 9-12 syllabi with subject-wise breakdowns.
- **Custom:** Create your own syllabus or import from other sources.

## First Steps After Setup

Now that you're set up, here is exactly what you should do next to get the most out of the app:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Your First Focus Session">
    1. Navigate to **Focus**
    2. Select a subject (or create one quickly)
    3. Choose **Pomodoro** or **Stopwatch** mode
    4. Hit **Start** and begin studying!
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Task">
    1. Go to **Tasks**
    2. Click the **+** button or use quick-add
    3. Enter task details with priority
    4. It appears on your Dashboard immediately
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Your Analytics">
    1. Open **Analytics**
    2. View Today's progress
    3. See your study time and tasks completed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore Community (Optional)">
    1. Visit **Community**
    2. Browse study groups
    3. Join challenges
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I don't see my exam in the list" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Use the **Custom** option to create your own syllabus, or contact support if you need a specific exam template added natively.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The app feels slow" icon="gauge">
    Enable **Potato Mode** in settings for better performance on older devices, or try clearing your browser cache.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I can't find a feature" icon="compass">
    Use the search bar in the header to find anything instantly, or check the sidebar navigation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
