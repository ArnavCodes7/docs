## Quick Start Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding">
    When you first open Isotope AI, you'll go through a quick setup:
    1. **Create your identity** - Choose your name and avatar
    2. **Academic info** - Select your grade and target exams
    3. **Study goals** - Set your daily study targets
    4. **Preferences** - Customize theme and notification settings
    5. **Join community** - Optionally connect with study groups
    
    <Tip>During onboarding, you can auto-load JEE or other exam syllabi to get started quickly!</Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Your Subjects">
    Navigate to the **Study** page to:
    - Create subjects (Math, Physics, Chemistry, etc.)
    - Add chapters and topics
    - Optionally load exam templates (JEE, NEET, CBSE)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Focusing">
    Head to **Focus** to:
    - Select a subject/chapter/topic
    - Choose session type (Pomodoro/Stopwatch)
    - Start your first session!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Tasks">
    Use **Tasks** to:
    - Add assignments and deadlines
    - Organize with priorities (P1-P4)
    - View in Board, List, Calendar, or Zen mode
  </Step>
</Steps>
