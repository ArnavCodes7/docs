## Tips & Tricks

### Using Analytics Effectively

1. **Check daily** - Monitor today's progress
2. **Review weekly** - Analyze trends
3. **Set goals** - Use data to set targets
4. **Identify patterns** - Find your best times
5. **Optimize** - Adjust based on insights

### Improving Metrics

- **Increase study time** - Schedule more sessions
- **Improve completion** - Break tasks into smaller steps
- **Build streaks** - Study every day, even briefly
- **Boost efficiency** - Remove distractions

### Leveraging AI Insights

- Read weekly summaries carefully
- Act on recommendations
- Track improvements over time
