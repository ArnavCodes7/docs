## Overview

The Focus page is designed with one goal: **help you study without distractions**.

The interface is divided into three logical sections:

1. **Left Panel:** Environment controls (Ambient sounds, Breathing, Goals).
2. **Center Stage:** The Timer and Session Type selector.
3. **Right Panel:** Stats, Daily Goals, and Session History.

## Timer Modes

Isotope AI offers two timer modes to suit different study styles.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pomodoro Mode üçÖ">
    The classic **Pomodoro Technique** for structured bursts of productivity.

    | Setting            | Default    | Customizable  |
    | ------------------ | ---------- | ------------- |
    | **Focus Duration** | 25 minutes | 5-120 minutes |
    | **Short Break**    | 5 minutes  | 1-30 minutes  |
    | **Long Break**     | 15 minutes | 5-60 minutes  |
    | **Cycle**          | 4 sessions | 2-10 sessions |

    **How it works:**

    1. Focus for set duration (e.g., 25 min).
    2. Take a short break (5 min).
    3. After 4 cycles, take a long break.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Stopwatch Mode ‚è±Ô∏è">
    Free-form timing for flexible study sessions.

    - **No preset duration** ‚Äì Study as long as you flow.
    - **Unlimited tracking** ‚Äì Track total time accurately.
    - **Marathon ready** ‚Äì Great for deep work.
    - **Exam Prep** ‚Äì Perfect for timed practice tests where you race against the clock.
  </Tab>

## Subject Selection

Use the **Hierarchical Selector** to pinpoint exactly what you are studying.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Subject">
    Choose the main subject (e.g., Physics, Chemistry, Math).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Chapter">
    Drill down to the chapter (e.g., Mechanics, Thermodynamics).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Topic">
    Pick the specific topic (e.g., Newton's Laws).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Task (Optional)">
    Link the session to a specific pending task.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Why It Matters

- Tracks time per topic for granular analytics.
- Helps AI understand your study patterns.
- Enables chapter-wise progress tracking.

## Zen Mode

Enter **Zen Mode** for complete immersion.

### What Happens

- Sidebar collapses.
- Header hides.
- Timer becomes the entire screen.
- Distractions vanish.

### How to use

1. Click the **expand icon** in the top right.
2. Or press `F` on your keyboard.
3. The interface transforms!

## Session Tracking

Every focus session is tracked to fuel your analytics.

### What's Recorded

- Start/End time & Duration
- Subject/Chapter/Topic details
- Session type (Study/Practice/etc)
- Questions attempted
- Efficiency rating

### Post-Session Summary

When you complete a session, you will see a summary popup:

1. **Rate your efficiency** - How focused were you?
2. **Add notes** - What did you cover?
3. **Rate productivity** - 1-5 stars.
4. **Mark tasks complete** - Link to specific tasks.
