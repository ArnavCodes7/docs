## How to Upgrade

### From the App

1. Click your **profile icon**
2. Go to **Settings**
3. Select **Subscription**
4. Choose your plan
5. Complete payment

### From the Subscription Page

1. Navigate to `/subscription`
2. View plan options
3. Select **Upgrade**
4. Follow payment steps

### Payment Methods

- Credit/Debit Cards
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Wallet (coming soon)

## Billing & Invoices

### Invoice History

Access all past invoices:
1. Go to Subscription
2. Click **Invoice History**
3. View/download invoices

### Invoice Details

Each invoice shows:
- Date
- Amount
- Plan
- Transaction ID
- Download link

### Payment Issues

If payment fails:
- Check card details
- Ensure sufficient funds
- Contact bank if needed
- Reach out to support

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel

1. Go to Subscription
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Access until period end

### What Happens

- Access continues until renewal date
- No further charges
- Data preserved
- Can resubscribe anytime

### Refund Policy

- 7-day money-back guarantee
- Contact support for issues
- Case-by-case for special situations
